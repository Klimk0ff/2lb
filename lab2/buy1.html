Books for children

Dunno on the Moon

There is not a single person in our country who does not know the name of Nikolai Nikolaevich Nosov. Several generations of young readers have already grown up on the books of this wonderful author, who, having matured, are happy to buy Nosov's books for their children and grandchildren. Together with them, they enthusiastically reread funny and instructive stories about dreamers and a living hat, funny stories about Vitya Maleev and Kolya Sinitsyn. The fabulous trilogy about Dunno and his friends is a classic of children's literature, it has forever entered its golden fund, becoming a national treasure of Russia. In the fantastic story "Dunno on the Moon" you will read about the space adventures of Dunno and his friends, who decided to go on a trip to the Moon. The book is beautifully illustrated by Vladimir Chellak.
